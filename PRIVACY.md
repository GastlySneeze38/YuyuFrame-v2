# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

## 1. Who we are

YuyuFrame is an open source peer-to-peer Minecraft launcher developed by Ghasty. The source code is available at https://github.com/gastlysneeze38/YuyuFrame-v2.

## 2. Data we collect

When you create a YuyuFrame account, we collect:

- **Username** (pseudo) — used to identify you in the launcher
- **Password** — stored in encrypted form only (hashed with Argon2 + salt, never stored in plain text)

We do **not** collect:
- Email addresses
- Real names
- Payment information
- IP addresses
- Any data related to peer-to-peer connections (P2P sessions are direct between players and not processed or stored by our servers)

## 3. How we use your data

Your username and encrypted password are used solely to authenticate you in the YuyuFrame launcher. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any third-party purposes.

## 4. Where your data is stored

Account data is stored on a private API server maintained by the YuyuFrame developer. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are always hashed using Argon2 with a unique salt before storage.

## 5. Your rights (RGPD / GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Object** to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please open an issue on our GitHub repository: https://github.com/gastlysneeze38/YuyuFrame-2/issues

## 6. Data retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you request account deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed.

## 7. Third parties

YuyuFrame does not share your data with any third party.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
